Overview

Ziva is an online administration network which is created to manage and keep track of status of the manholes, purpose of the maintenance hole and location.

Uncovered manholes become horrid complications, especially during the rainy season people fall into one of these holes which are filled with rainwater accidentally.

Ziva is an online manhole administration network that alerts the appropriate authorities regarding the manholes which are left open even after serving its purpose of installation. Ziva can be used with at most reliance to ensure the padding of the holes post-haste the work.

Ziva helps to save many worthy lives from accidents due to improper management of these holes.

Social Issue

Uncovered manholes are posing a great threat to commuters in city. Passers-by are at high risk of facing mishaps while crossing these manholes that are either damaged or kept open by the sanitation workers. The situation worsens during the rainy season. Even a slight down pour causes the manholes to overflow.
